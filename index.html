Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Xwork

How does the zero-commission launch work?

For the first six months after signup, you pay 0% in platform fees. This gives you time to build your reputation and client base without any upfront costs. After the free period, we charge a competitive 5% commission rate.

How are freelancers verified on Xwork?

We use a comprehensive verification process including identity verification, skill assessments, portfolio reviews, and AI-powered background checks. Verified freelancers receive a blue badge and higher visibility in search results.

What payment methods do you support?

We support major payment gateways including Stripe, PayPal, Payoneer, and local payment methods in 50+ countries. All payments are processed securely through our escrow system.

How does the AI matching system work?

Our AI analyzes freelancer profiles, past project success, client reviews, and job requirements to create intelligent matches. The system learns from successful projects to improve matching accuracy over time.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 customer support through live chat, email, and phone. Premium plan users get priority support and dedicated account managers for personalized assistance.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you won't be charged for the next cycle.
